Updated Product Requirements for Bashir Flour Shop

Add the following requirements to the product display system:

Measurement Unit Display:

All wheat products must display their measurement unit as "1 Maan = 40 Kg" prominently on product cards

Wheat product prices should be calculated and displayed per "Maan" unit

Flour products should continue to use standard kilogram/pound units as appropriate

Bilingual Product Details:

Each product must have complete bilingual descriptions in both English and Urdu

Product names should be displayed in both languages with proper RTL/LTR formatting

Product descriptions should include detailed specifications in both English and Urdu

Measurement units should be translated appropriately for each language context

Product Card Modifications:

Update ProductCard component to show bilingual product information

Add a clear measurement unit indicator for wheat products

Display pricing information with the appropriate unit (Maan for wheat, Kg for flour)

Implement conditional rendering to show wheat-specific measurement info only for wheat products

Product Data Structure:

Add bilingual description fields to product objects: descriptionEn and descriptionUr

Include measurement unit specification in product data

Add price per unit display logic

Admin Product Form:

Update ProductForm to include separate input fields for English and Urdu descriptions

Add measurement unit selection (Maan/Kg) based on product category

Include validation for bilingual content

Price Calculation:

For wheat products: Display price per Maan (1 Maan = 40 Kg)

For flour products: Display price per standard unit (Kg or Lb)

Clearly indicate the measurement unit next to all prices

UI/UX Considerations:

Ensure bilingual text switches properly when language changes

Maintain consistent formatting for measurement units

Provide clear visual distinction between wheat and flour products

Support RTL text rendering for Urdu product descriptions
